About Rhapsody'14

Rhapsody is the Annual Spring Cultural Festival of IIT Roorkee. Started in 2008 as Rave and 2010 as TaRanG, Rhapsody boasts about the glorious past of its ancestors.


Rhapsody strives to bring about a change in the society.
Rhapsody is the ultimate platform to fulfill your dreams.
Rhapsody is a team with pedigree and passion.
Rhapsody is an experience.

After the glorious success in past years as TaRanG, one of the most awaited festival of IIT Roorkee is back again. Get ready to witness rivalry, companionship and enjoyment in a whole new dimension and be prepared to behold an ultra colossal Rhapsody'14..
Rhapsody'14 has something for everyone.. from the most hardcore nerd, to the dudes and dudettes.
So come one come all, experience Fungeneering at Rhapsody'14 - "Feel the Beat".

Thahake

The essence of contemporary Indian Poetry coupled with the blend of wit and sarcasm provided by a stand up performance does make for a stimulating experience. Be prepared for fits of laughter as renowned hindi poets and stand up comedians assend center stage and enthrill everyone with their command of the language as well as dosses of hilarity under Thahake, the Hasya Kavi Sammelan under Rhapsody. Chuckles at peculiar habits of our celebrities, potshots at the flailing conditions of our political systems and light hearted observations on student liffe, all this coupled with deep symbolism and intricately composed rhyming verses. Apart from being an entertaining spectacle, this event also aims to revive and promote contemporary Indian Poetry.

Prayaas

The highlight of Rhapsody would be the essential focus attributed to social service through an unprecedented endeavour, Prayaas. Completely based on social upliftment of the needy, Prayaas has the sole aim of empowering the specially abled section of the society and to bridge the gap between the privileged and the underprivileged section of the society. Apart from adding a social dimension to an already technologically sound image of the institute., Prayaas would imbibe the essence of socio-corporate responsibility. Under it, Prayaas would comprise a myriad of events held in association with some of the most renowned NGO’s in the country, whose sole aims remain bettering of underprivileged lives. Prayaas would comprise the following events:

  • Aasha
  • Kiran
  • Jeevan
